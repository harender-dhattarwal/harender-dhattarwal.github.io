About Harender

Harender S. Dhattarwal is a postdoctoral associate in the Department of Chemistry and Chemical Biology at Rutgers University. Harender received his Ph.D. in Chemistry from Indian Institute of Techonology Delhi, India. During that time, he conducted research in the lab of Professor Hemant Kashyap, focusing on the the classical and ab-initio molecular dynamics simulation of modern electrolytes in confinements and near interfaces. Harender's current work in Rick's group focuses on energy storage materials.
